The Mission

A living border world — not a generic flight trainer.

DroneSym is a PC-based, GPU-accelerated simulation built on Unreal Engine 5. It reproduces the exact flight behaviour of in-service indigenous platforms, populates the world with adaptive AI enemies, friendly NPC ground crew and animal NPCs, and drops operators into real heightmap terrain of India's border sectors — at any hour, in any weather.

Every scenario is an event-driven system: fire a flare and the village dogs bark; fly too low over cattle and the herd scatters; trigger a jamming event and your HUD goes to static. No scripted railroads. A different experience every time.

Artificial Intelligence

Not scripted.
Adaptive.

AI is not a single feature — it is a stack of six cooperating kits. The enemy operator watches your patrol pattern and launches in your gap. The friendly tutor notices your gimbal was 20° off and tells you why you lost the contact. The goats scatter when you fly overhead.

All AI runs fully offline, on-device. No cloud dependency. Data-sovereign. Training-only — never wired to any real fire-control system.

BHV

Drone Behaviour Kit (Friendly)

Autonomous modes — waypoint, terrain-follow, obstacle avoid, return-to-home, failsafe. Human-on-the-loop supervised autonomy.

RED

AI Adversary / Enemy Kit

Hostile drones fly plausible smuggling profiles. Ground adversaries evade, conceal and adapt. Lookout network relays patrol positions. Difficulty scales from predictable to reinforcement-learning adaptive.

TUT

AI Co-pilot & Intelligent Tutor

AI wingman in multi-UAV scenarios. Adaptive tutor adjusts difficulty per trainee. Automated debrief explains exactly what went wrong and why.

NPC

NPC / Population & Animal Kit

Civilian clutter — vehicles, people, livestock, birds. Thermal signatures designed to train discrimination. Every animal reacts to drone proximity, light and sound.

ATR

Sensor-AI / ATR Teaming

Simulated automatic target recognition on the EO/IR feed — with deliberate false alarms. Operators learn human-AI teaming and when to override the machine.

GEN

Procedural Generation & Analytics

Infinite scenario variation. Performance analytics, readiness scoring, cohort comparison. Random seed sharing so instructors can issue identical conditions to a whole class.
